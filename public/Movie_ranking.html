Check Out all time best movies according to Abdul Kadir.

Below are My top 5 movie recommendations.

Fighter

Description: Directed by Siddharth Anand, "Fighter" is a high-octane action drama featuring Hrithik Roshan, Deepika Padukone, and Anil Kapoor. The film delves into the lives of air force pilots, promising intense aerial sequences and a gripping storyline.
Release Date: January 25, 2024.

Chandu Champion

Description: This sports-centric film by Kabir Khan is based on a true story. It follows the inspiring journey of Chandu, highlighting themes of resilience and triumph against odds. The movie commenced its journey with a mahurat shot in London.
Release Date: June 14, 2024.

Stree 2

Description: The sequel to the 2018 horror-comedy hit, "Stree 2" brings back Shraddha Kapoor, Rajkummar Rao, and Pankaj Tripathi. It promises more chills and laughs, continuing the story of the ghostly Stree.
Release Date: August 30, 2024.

Jigra

Description: Alia Bhatt stars in and produces this film directed by Vasan Bala. While the plot details are under wraps, it's expected to be a powerful narrative showcasing Alia's acting prowess.
Release Date: September 27, 2024.

Bhool Bhulaiyaa 3

Description: Kartik Aaryan reprises his role as Rooh baba in this anticipated sequel directed by Anees Bazmee. Following the success of the previous installments, this film is expected to blend horror and comedy effectively.
Release Date: November 1, 2024.

These films span a variety of genres, from action and sports dramas to horror-comedies, promising a diverse and exciting cinematic experience for Bollywood fans in 2024
